King Woman'Fried rice and Chicken

This fried rice is a classic nigerian fried rice dish packed with veggies and a flavourful mix of spices. perfect for parties and Sunday delicacies

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large egg

  • 1 tablespoon water

  • 1 tablespoon butter

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

  • 1 medium onion, chooped

  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold

  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

STEPS TO COOK

  1. Gather all ingredients


  2. Beat egg and water together in small bowl. melt butter in a large skillet or work over medium-low heat; add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes. remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set aside


  3. heat oil in the same skillet. Add onion: cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes. stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes; stir in shredded egg. Serve hot
