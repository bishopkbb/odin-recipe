Kingwoman's Akara recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 cups of beans

  • 1 handful pepper

  • 1 medium sized onion

  • 2 cubes of maggi

  • 1 drizzle of salt

  • 1/4 cup of water

  • Pap(your prefered measurement)

  • Sugar(to your taste)

  • 3/4 bottle of vegetable oil

  • Milk(to your tatste

    STEPS TO COOK

  1. Wash and peel the beans

  2. Place in a blender and blend to paste with pepper; Make sure you add water while blending

  3. After blending mix properly(king woman used mortar to mix her own) add salt and maggi cubes then mix well


  4. Place a deep frying panon fire and add vegetable oil

  5. Wait for the oil to heat up and scoop the akara paste into the pan

  6. Let the akara balls float up and turn it. Make sure each side is golden brown


  7. Wait till it turns golden brown, remove and place in a sieve to drain oil


  8. Repeat process till all batter paste is finished

  9. For the pap, empty the pap into a bowl, add water little water and mix till all the particles are dossolved. Make sure the paste is not too watery

  10. Boil water at maximum boiling point and pour the hot water gradually into the paste,mix the pap paste as you pour the hot water until it thickens

  11. Add sugar and milk to your taste and mix well

  12. serve hot with Akara
